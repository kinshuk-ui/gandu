Welcome to
Gangotri School

Gangotri English Boarding Secondary School, Dhanusha was established in 1992 AD (2049 BS) as a private educational institution, which is situated in Hariharpur, Dhanusha, Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education. It offers educational programs from PG to grade 10 and Plus Two (Class 11 and 12) in the Management, and Education streams.
Gangotri English Boarding Secondary School, Dhanusha provides up to secondary level education with moderate fee structures and also provides a scholarship scheme for the deserving students.

Salient Features:- Science Lab, Sports, Computer Lab, Multimedia, Journal, Counseling, Scholarships, Classrooms, Conference Hall, Dance, Music, Tour, ECA, Drinking Water,etc.
